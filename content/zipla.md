---
title: Jump!
---
A browser-based 2D platformer built with HTML5 Canvas. Run, jump across platforms, collect coins and reach the flag to clear each level. Features tight physics (gravity, jumping, AABB collision), a camera that follows the player, keyboard and on-screen touch controls, and level progression with best times saved. Playable on desktop and mobile.

<!--more-->

Technologies: JavaScript, HTML5 Canvas, CSS
