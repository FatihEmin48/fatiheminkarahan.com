---
title: Gold Mine
---
A browser-based idle / incremental game. Click to mine gold, hire miners and machines, automate your production, and grow your mining empire with upgrades. Costs scale up with each purchase, and generators produce gold automatically every second. The game auto-saves and your miners keep working while you're away (offline earnings). Playable on desktop and mobile.

Technologies: JavaScript, HTML5, CSS
