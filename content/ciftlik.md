---
title: Farm
---
A browser-based farming game. Choose a crop, plant it on your plots, and harvest it for coins once it grows in real time. Reinvest your earnings into faster, more valuable crops and buy new plots to expand your farm. Growth is time-based, so your crops keep maturing while you're away — come back to harvest them. The game auto-saves. Playable on desktop and mobile.

Technologies: JavaScript, HTML5, CSS
